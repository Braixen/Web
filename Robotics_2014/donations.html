FIRST Robotics Website

If you wish to sponsor or donate to Pittsford Panthers Robotics team 3181, please contact Brian Holliday at brian_holliday@pittsford.monroe.edu.


When making out checks to Pittsford Robotics, please make them out to Rochester Community Robotics (RCR). RCR is a non-profit organization which helps FIRST teams in the Rochester area with fundraising and financial advising. Please use the memo field to signify that you would like to donate specifically to Team 3181.
