Attention Judges!

Our website is not only home to the front of our team, but we've done a lot to help our team out! Here's a quick list of little extra things we've done.



Attendance FormWe use this form to take our team's attendance and keep track of who's at each meeting.
 
Sub-Team ReportsA way for the mentors to know what happens during each meeting and track our progress.
 
Paint The PnatherA fun, simple flash game that keeps people coming back!
 
Emergency MessagingA form that sends an email and a text message to every parent, member, and mentor on the team to inform them of sudden schedule changes or important updates.
Running PantherAn animation of the panther running across the header.
 
Team NewsletterOur monthly team newsletter is distributed here.
 
Wiki / FilesharingA team wiki with information about the members, the team, and is used for file sharing and discussion.
