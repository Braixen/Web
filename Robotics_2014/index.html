Pittsford Robotics 2014

We are the Pittsford Panthers FIRST Robotics Competition Team 3181. We are made up of students from Pittsford Mendon and Sutherland High Schools. We have been a part of FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) since 2010. Our goal this year is to be the best.
